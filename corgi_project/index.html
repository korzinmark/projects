About me

My name is Simon! I am a Cardigan Welsh Corgi dog. Our breed originated from the UK, so I speak some English, but this is not certain. Most of all I like rabbit ears and cottage cheese. Also, I'm a little bit lazy despite being 2 years old, but my owner does everything to fight it.

About owner

My name is Sergey! And I am the owner of this beautiful wonder. I walk with Simon 3 times a day, for a total of about 3 hours. From time to time I take him to special areas where we play with him and generally fight the sloth syndrome. Also, at these sites, he learns to communicate with other dogs, which is very important in our modern world.
